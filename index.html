Hi! my name is Bin Han

I received Bachelor degree in Civil Environmental and Architectural Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering
from Korea University. I received Master dgree in Computer Science and Engineering at the Korea University.
My advisor was Professor. Gerard Jounghyun Kim. Currently, I am a research assistant at Center for Artificial Intelligence
in the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST). My main research interests are Humean Computer Interaction,
