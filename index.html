Bin Han

Hi! I'm Bin Han. Currently, I am in my first year as a CS Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California. I am interested in Human-Computer Interaction, Affective Computing, Virtual Reality, and Accessibility.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil, Environmental, and Architectural Engineering, as well as in Computer Science and Engineering, both from Korea University. I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the same institution, under the guidance of Professor Gerard Jounghyun Kim. Following my Master's, I contributed as a research assistant at the Center for Artificial Intelligence at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST).

My dream is to create a future where people with disabilities can communicate and participate in society equally, without discrimination.
