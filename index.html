I’m a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California, where I work with Professor Jonathan Gratch in the Affective Computing Group. My research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction, Affective Computing, Virtual Reality, and Virtual Agent.

Previously, I earned my BS and MS in Computer Science from Korea University, where I was advised by Professor Gerard Jounghyun Kim. I also worked as a research intern at the Center for Artificial Intelligence of the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) in the Mixed Reality Lab.

In Summer 2025, I worked at Apple in the Vision Products Group as a Machine Learning Engineer Intern.
