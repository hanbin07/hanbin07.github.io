My vision is to develop technologies that ensure every individual, regardless of their abilities, can fully and equally participate in society.

Bin Han

I am in my second year as a Computer Science Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California, advised by Professor Jonathan Gratch. My interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Affective Computing, Virtual Reality, and Accessibility.

I hold a double major in Civil Engineering and Computer Science from Korea University. I earned my Master's in Computer Science at the same university, advised by Professor Gerard Jounghyun Kim at the Digital Experience Lab. Afterwards, I worked as a research assistant at the Center for Artificial Intelligence of the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST), at the Mixed Reality Lab .
