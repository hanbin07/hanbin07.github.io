About Me

Bin Han

Hi! my name is Bin Han

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Civil, Environmental, and Architectural Engineering, as well as in Computer Science and Engineering, from Korea University. I also received my Master's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University, under the guidance of Professor. Gerard Jounghyun Kim. After completing my Master's, I served as a research assistant at the Center for Artificial Intelligence at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST). Currently, I am in my 1st year as a CS Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California. My primary research interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Spatial Computing (encompassing Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality), Affective Computing, Accessibility, and Deep Learning.

I dream of a future where people with disabilities can communicate and participate equally in society without discrimination.
